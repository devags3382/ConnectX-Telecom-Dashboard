## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by telecom companies. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones. This project analyzes customer churn data to identify patterns, trends, and factors that influence customer attrition.

The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, service usage, contract types, payment methods, tenure, and revenue, helping businesses develop effective retention strategies and improve customer satisfaction.

---

# Problem Statement

Analyze and visualize customer churn data for a telecom company and explore customer demographics, offers, contract types, payment methods, and service usage.

The objective is to identify the key factors contributing to customer churn and provide actionable insights that can help reduce customer attrition and improve business performance.

---

# Objectives

* Calculate and analyze customer churn rate.
* Understand customer demographics such as age and gender.
* Analyze customer tenure and its relationship with churn.
* Evaluate contract types and payment methods.
* Study customer revenue and service usage patterns.
* Identify key drivers of customer churn.
* Provide recommendations to improve customer retention.

# Dashboard Features

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes:

* Total Customers
* Churned Customers
* Churn Rate (%)

---

## Visualizations Included

### 1. Customer Churn Rate

**Chart Type:** Pie Chart / Donut Chart

Displays:

* Percentage of customers who have churned
* Percentage of customers who have stayed

Purpose:

* Quickly understand overall customer retention performance.

---

### 2. Customer Demographics Analysis

**Chart Type:** Bar Charts / Distribution Charts

Displays:

* Gender Distribution
* Age Distribution

Purpose:

* Understand the composition of the customer base.

---

### 3. Customer Tenure Distribution

**Chart Type:** Histogram (Column Chart)

Displays:

* Distribution of customer tenure in months
* Churned customers highlighted using a separate color

Purpose:

* Identify churn trends based on customer tenure.

---

### 4. Contract Type Analysis

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

Displays:

* Monthly Contract
* One-Year Contract
* Two-Year Contract

Churned customers are highlighted separately.

Purpose:

* Determine which contract types are most associated with customer churn.

---

### 5. Payment Method Distribution

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart / Pie Chart

Displays:

* Credit Card
* Bank Transfer
* Electronic Check
* Mailed Check
* Other payment methods

Purpose:

* Analyze customer payment preferences.

---

### 6. Total Charges vs Customer Tenure

**Chart Type:** Scatter Plot

Displays:

* X-Axis: Tenure in Months
* Y-Axis: Total Charges
* Churned customers highlighted in a different color

Purpose:

* Examine the relationship between customer tenure and total charges.

---

### 7. Total Revenue Distribution

**Chart Type:** Histogram

Displays:

* Distribution of total revenue generated by customers

Purpose:

* Identify high-value and low-value customer segments.

# Key Insights

## Customer Churn Rate

* Approximately **26% of customers have churned**.
* Nearly one out of every four customers leaves the company, indicating a significant retention challenge.

---

## Contract Preference

* Customers highly prefer the **Monthly Contract Plan**.
* Monthly contract subscribers exhibit higher churn rates compared to long-term contract customers.

---

## Tenure and Charges Relationship

* A positive relationship exists between tenure and total charges.
* Customers who stay longer generate higher revenue for the company.

---

## Customer Age Analysis

* Customers who churn tend to be older.
* The average age of churned customers is approximately **50 years**.

---

## Revenue Contribution

* Long-term customers contribute significantly more revenue.
* Customer retention directly impacts profitability.

# Recommendations

## 1. Develop Products for Older Customers

Since churned customers are generally older:

* Introduce senior-friendly plans.
* Offer age-specific benefits and loyalty rewards.
* Create simplified service packages.

---

## 2. Personalized Customer Offers

Conduct customer surveys to understand customer expectations and preferences.

Based on survey results:

* Provide personalized discounts.
* Recommend suitable service packages.
* Offer customized upgrade plans.

---

## 3. Improve Customer Experience

Increase customer satisfaction through:

* Better customer support services.
* Faster issue resolution.
* Improved self-service options.

---

## 4. Strengthen Network Infrastructure

Enhance service quality by:

* Establishing additional network towers.
* Improving signal strength and coverage.
* Reducing network interruptions.

---

## 5. Customer Retention Programs

Implement:

* Loyalty programs
* Renewal incentives
* Exclusive benefits for long-term customers
* Referral rewards

These initiatives can reduce churn and improve customer lifetime value.
